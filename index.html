Frequently Asked Questions

How to cancel?

You can cancel your booking by contacting our support team at least 24 hours in advance.

Is there any vacancy?

Availability depends on the season. Please check our website or contact us for current openings.

What are the payment methods?

We accept credit/debit cards, mobile payments, and bank transfers.

How to claim coupon codes?

Enter your coupon code at checkout or present it at the front desk during payment.

What are the age requirements?

Guests must be at least 18 years old to book a room. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.
